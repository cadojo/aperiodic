What does aperiodic mean?

The URL of this site is aperiodic.dev. This is my attempt at a play on words! I’m a periodic developer — my career primarily relates to aerospace engineering, but I write scientific software frequently to serve that purpose. I also studied dynamics and controls, in that context aperiodicity refers to a non-periodic system. The term aperiodic relates to my relationship with scientific software development, and a field I’m passionate about. For only $12 on Google’s URL registrator, that’s a steal!
